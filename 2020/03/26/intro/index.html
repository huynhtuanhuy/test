This is my first blog ever !! Even though I am just still beginner in programming, I already have two year of intensive coding and I hope you will enjoy the experiences I have to Share

I gotta say I didnt start programming because all of the sayings such as “Job warranty”, “This is a super hot suject”, “You will be super rich”,…

It all began while I was playing computer games at home, chilling after finishing the intense highschool entrance test. Just as my screen was grey in the middle of a League of Legends match, I got a call from my best friend.

“Wanna join a coding class? Its about to start”, he said

Then all the sudden, the then ever-rising competitiveness with my friends (I used to think whatever they knew I must learn it as well) had urged me to abandoned my game, which caused me to drop some rank points :(, and headed straight to the class in HUS

And my passion for coding all started in this website

This is a common website for most informatics majors in HUS and since I took this summer class, I sneaked an account on Codefun.

My first lines of code, pretty stupid common error right :)) But I know as a beginner then I was sympathized by most

After that it was some of the most passionated days of my life. I could feel every problem solved was exciting. The minute I saw each green tick on each problem was a reward for all the instructions in our secret book, all the stackoverflow, all the lines of code we minutely copied online but not understanding why it didnt work.

It was quite a hectic summer but when the new school year about to come, I left all the passion for coding behind and focus on studying Math (a thing that I still both regret and be grateful for). I was in the National Mathematics Team twice but got the honorable prize twice, quite ashamed :))

Then, the passion for coding in me arised again when I saw this really cool Codeathon from Techkids (Mindx now) and I was realyl grateful of seeing that advertisement. I immediately signed up for the 2-day codecamp (sounds weird) but it was 2 days of 24 hours coding and learning about the thing that make me love coding today web-dev.

In the end, after the successful camp (we won first place :)), my passion for web-dev and coding in general was ignited again… Here are some of the first html, css and javascript I designed for the codeathon:

Pretty cool right !

In summary, I studied different languages such as python, java, c++ (for the sake of learning algorithms), and my specialities are javascript and React so you will expect many posts related to these 2 languages.

Even though I am not any professional developers or informatic major students, I believe on this blog you will find many helpful advice and experience that I gathered during my 2 years of learning web-dev.

Hope you enjoy it!
